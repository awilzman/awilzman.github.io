Grounded
<!--more-->


>Close your mouth.<br>
>Open your lungs.<br>
>Breathe in,<br>
>basement entrance.<br>


>Release clearly,<br>
>but quietly,<br>
>and count the number<br>
>of interesting things<br>
>that you see.


>Nothing can hurt you,<br>
>not pain, nor grief;<br>
>you live in your own world.
