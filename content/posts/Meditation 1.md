Grounded
<!--more-->

Close your mouth.

Open your lungs.

Breathe in,

basement entrance.




Release clearly,

but quietly,

and count the number

of interesting things

that you see.



Nothing can hurt you,

not pain, nor grief;

you live in your own world.
